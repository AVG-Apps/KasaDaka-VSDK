Create or edit your fertilizer

To add a new fertilizer into the database you need to add all the information in the form. Please add the name, description, audio file url, the image url of the fertilizer and the related crop and weather condtion.

Warning, it is important that the audio files are in WAVE format. The WAVE file must have a sample rate of 8KHZ, 16 bit, mono, codec: PCM 16 LE (s16I). Otherwise the voice application cannot play the audio file.
