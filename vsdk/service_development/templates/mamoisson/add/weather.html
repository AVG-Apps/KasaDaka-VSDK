Weather condition

To add a new weather condition you need to add the name and an url of the picture of the weather condition into the form. Please add the name, the audio file url, the image url of the crop.

Warning, it is important that the audio files are in WAVE format. The WAVE file must have a sample rate of 8KHZ, 16 bit, mono, codec: PCM 16 LE (s16I). Otherwise the voice application cannot play the audio file.
